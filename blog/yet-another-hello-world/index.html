Yet Another 'Hello World'

By Yann Hulot March 31, 2016 Comment Tweet Like +1

Welcome

This will be my introductory post, I hope you will enjoy my company during this journey.

It all started about 2.5 years ago when I first came to Australia. Back then, I use to work in construction as a labourer.

Having trouble finding a job through the regular channels such as Gumtree or Seek, I dreamed of a way to be contacted by employers with available casual jobs instead of me having to go through many job offers and applying to all of them.

Not having the skills to build it myself, I ironically posted an ad on Gumtree in order to find like minded people who would be available to work with me.

From this idea was born JobCompanion. I believed in the idea but my lack of funding and the length of development made this project come to a standstill. I have now decided to stop working on this project but maybe go back to it later on after landing a full time job. I will go through more detailed explanations about the failure of this project in a future post.

Nonetheless, it has been a great learning experience and made me discover that I wanted to work in the tech industry.

Because I did not have time to go back to school to learn web development, I started using MOOCS such as TeamTreeHouse, CodeCademy and FreeCodeCamp. Eventually after completing a dozen front-end projects, I decided to find an internship in a startup. A month ago, I decided to join Modhub and have been learning Ruby On rails as well as developing the user interface of their support site.

This blog will be my diary and I will use it as well to post the various solutions to all the FreeCodeCamp challenges. You can find the solutions directly on my github.

My goal is to find full-time employment in a web agency or in a startup by the end of June.

In the meantime, I will keep you updated about my learning process and my daily activities at Modhub.

keep on coding.
